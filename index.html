The Origin Story

From Thailand to the Tallow Pot.

It started with a trip to Thailand and a discovery of how potent botanical care can be. As a restaurant professional, I knew how to source the best ingredients—so I applied that "Chef's Logic" to the shower.

Smart Aleck is about zero synthetics, farm-fresh goat milk, and a base of restaurant-grade beef tallow. It's chef-inspired skincare that doesn't take itself too seriously.
