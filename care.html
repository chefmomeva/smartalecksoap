Soap Care Guide

Don't let your investment melt away. Handcrafted soap needs a little respect.

1. High Tallow = High Thirst

Because our soaps are loaded with restaurant-grade tallow and goat milk, they are incredibly moisturizing—but they also love to soak up water. If you leave your bar in a puddle, it will turn into a "Smart Aleck Slushie."

2. Use a Draining Dish

The secret to a bar that lasts 3x longer? Airflow. Use a soap dish with ridges or holes that allow the bar to dry out completely between uses.

3. Keep it out of the Spray

Store your soap on a ledge where the shower head isn't constantly hitting it. Only get it wet when you're ready to get clean.

Pro Tip

"If you're using our Shampoo bar, let it dry standing up on its side. More surface area = faster drying = more washes for your money."
