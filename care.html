Soap Care

01. Airflow is Everything

Handcrafted tallow soap is rich and moisturizing, which means it loves to hold onto water. If it sits in a puddle, it’s history. Use a draining soap dish with ridges or slats.

02. High & Dry

Keep your bar on a high ledge away from the constant spray of the shower head. Only get it wet when it's actually "go time."

03. The Shampoo Secret

For our Mallow Glide bars: let them dry standing up on their thinnest side. It maximizes the air contact and keeps the bar hard and ready for the next wash.

Chef's Rule

"If the soap stays soft, you're doing it wrong. Let it cure between uses and it'll treat you right for weeks."

The Smart Aleck FAQ

I. The Science

Why Tallow?

It’s bio-compatible skincare. Tallow is nearly identical to the molecular structure of human skin oils. While plant oils often sit on the surface, tallow actually sinks in to nourish. We upcycle restaurant-grade fat that we render ourselves.

Why Goat Milk?

Water is a filler; goat milk is an ingredient. It’s loaded with Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs) that gently break down dead skin cells and minerals that feed your skin.

Essential Oils vs. Synthetics

We use 100% pure Essential Oils. "Fragrance" is a legal loophole that allows companies to hide thousands of chemicals under one word. We’d rather smell like real plants than a laboratory.
